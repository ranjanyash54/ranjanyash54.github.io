I am a third year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Florida. My research interests are in data-driven simulation for intelligent transporation, scientific simulation and world models. I aim to apply generative modelling techniques to learn the latent interactions in the data to simulate new worlds that are grounded in the data. I finished my undergradute from BITS Pilani, majoring in Computer Science.
